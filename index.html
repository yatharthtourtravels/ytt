About Maraog

A Hidden Himalayan Gem Awaiting You

Maraog Village, located in Chaupal, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, is one of the most beautiful hidden destinations in the Himalayas. Surrounded by dense deodar forests, apple orchards, breathtaking mountain landscapes, and traditional Himachali culture — Maraog offers an authentic and peaceful experience far away from crowded tourist spots.

🍎
Rich Apple-Growing Heritage Explore scenic orchards and discover local farming traditions deeply rooted in Himachali culture.
🌲
Dense Deodar Forests & Valleys Walk through untouched forests, breathe pure mountain air, and discover hidden viewpoints.
🏛️
Cultural & Spiritual Significance Ancient Shiv temples, traditional Himachali architecture, and living village customs await.
📸
Photographer's Paradise Breathtaking vistas, forest trails, village life — every frame tells a Himalayan story.
